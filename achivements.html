About us

The education program of the “Street School Mission” work ensure education for out-of-school children and to empower communities to collaborate effectively with the government to ensure quality education to their children.The key objective are to ensure access to quality education for the rural and tribal children in the group of 3 to 14, to increase community demand for the quality education and deepen understanding of the need thereof, to empower youth with agency and confidence to be community leaders.“AAINA PARYAVARAN VIKAS SANSTHAN” works in areas where children face hurdles in accessing quality education.
